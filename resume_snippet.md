# Hire Roofer
**App Store & Play Store**
*A dedicated marketplace that connects homeowners, businesses, contractors, and professional roofers.*

- Developed job posting, quote management, and interactive map-based job discovery features
- Integrated real-time messaging, secure payments, and document/certification management
- Implemented comprehensive authentication including Google Sign-In, Apple Sign-In, and OTP

**App URLs:**
- **Google Play Store:** [Hire Roofer - Android](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.hireroofer.app)
- **Apple App Store:** [Hire Roofer - iOS](https://apps.apple.com/au/app/hire-roofer/id6776122240)
